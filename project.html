SOULRESONANCE - GEMA REBANA

An art-themed embedded system designed to modernize traditional music accessibility. This project features a wearable electronic rebana utilizing a tactile acrylic interface that generates authentic sounds upon impact. Equipped with a dual-player mode, SOULRESONANCE aims to democratize cultural arts, allowing users from all backgrounds to experience and play traditional instruments anywhere, removing the need to visit specific art centers.
